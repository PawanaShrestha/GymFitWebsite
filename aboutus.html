ABOUT US

With the tag-line "Fitness is a POWER", GYM Fit is a one stop solution for all your fitness requirements. Keeping in mind the busy schedule of today's world, we have flexibility according to the customer's choice. The GYM Fit offers various trainings such as yoga, zumba, cardio, kick-boxing and basic-gym. We have highly qualified trainers in their particular fields ready to train you to become the next professional at your convinience. We also offer package trainings like absolute beginner, train for marathon, intro to weight loss and total body boot camp. The package can be altered as per your requirements.


Not limited to only the exercises, GYM Fit also has a shopping facility where you can get all your required nutrition and equipments. Become a member of GYM Fit and experience all our great facilities. Once you are a member of GYM Fit, you get a lifetime service. Make yourself our priority by joining the GYM Fit family today!!
